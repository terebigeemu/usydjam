# The Fridge Stole My Lunch!
### submitted for the USYD Game Jam 2026

> You are not so different from others in your corporate 9-5 office. 
> 
> You, unwillingly or not, become a victim (or a perpetrator) of office politics.
> 
> You yearn for a promotion and to be in the C-suite one day.
> 
> You meet your KPIs because you can't lose this job.
> 
> You're a dime-a-dozen in your typical corporation.
> 
> But there is one thing which sets you apart.
> 
> You are a fridge.

## How to play

- This game primarily uses mouse clicks as its key input mechanism. 
- To skip the cutscene, click anywhere on the screen.
- Clicking on items will move them between your stash and fridge.
- Hide items in the stash so employees can't take them.
- Use the shop to buy and sell items.
- Hover over items to see their effects, and gain affinity points by leaving food that matches the likes of different employees.
- Higher cost items more give more affinity points.
- Gain 20 affinity points with certain employees to get a chance at being promoted.
- Employees with negative affinity points will fire you.

## Key objective

Your objective is to maximise your affinity scores with employees who can promote you, without falling out of favour with employees who can fire you. (Unfortunately, as a humble office fridge, you don't exactly know who's responsible for procurement in the company!)
